Fabrem

Fabrem is one of Edemia's oldest surviving-and most powerful- civilisations. Its founding by King Sovreign Dreamglace marked the beginning of the Age of Kings, and the longstanding tradition to track time using shifts in rulership.

Fabrem has grown and shrunk many times over its history, but has been able to maintain its status as an empire since archaeic times. In large in large part due to advanced communication rituals and the ability of the majority usogami army to feed itself without physical rations.
